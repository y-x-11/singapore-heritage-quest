# Heritage Card QR Codes

Print these QR codes on heritage cards for field trips. Each card unlocks one mission when scanned.

Generate QR images at [qr-code-generator.com](https://www.qr-code-generator.com/) using the **exact JSON string** below.

## Card Design Tips

- Use A6 or postcard size
- Include location emoji and character name on the front
- Print QR on the back
- Laminate for field trip durability
